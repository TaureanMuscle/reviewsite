My Favorite Movies of the Decade

By far the best of the decade

Parasite was named Best Picture by the Academy in 2020. I couldn't agree more, this film is a force. It grips you and doesn't let go!

Once Upon a Time in Hollywood garnered a ton of buzz and a few Oscar nominations. Brad Pitt won for best supporting Actor for his role in Tarantino's Epic.

One foot above the competition in 2019 was the masterpiece that is The Joker. This film is disturbingly beautiful. Striking a chord with movie goers worldwide to the tune of a billion dollar box office.
